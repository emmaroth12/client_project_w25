UMSI Course Catalog

Your Guide to Information Analysis and User Experience Courses

The UMSI Course Catalog provides an in-depth look at the diverse courses available to students within the School of Information, focusing on two core areas: Information Analysis and User Experience (UX). These categories offer foundational and advanced courses designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge.

Course Categories:

Information Analysis

Courses in Information Analysis concentrate on data handling, analysis techniques, and interpretation to drive informed decision-making. You'll learn about:

  • Data Collection and Management: Techniques for gathering and organizing data efficiently.
  • Statistical Analysis: Applying statistical methods to analyze data sets.
  • Data Visualization: Creating visual representations to communicate data insights clearly.

User Experience (UX)

User Experience courses emphasize designing intuitive and effective user interactions with technology. Areas of focus include:

  • User Research: Understanding user needs and behaviors through research methods.
  • Interface Design: Designing user interfaces that are both functional and appealing.
  • Usability Testing: Techniques for testing and improving user interfaces.

Planning Your Courses:

Use the following tips for selecting the right courses and planning your academic path:

  1. Review prerequisites to ensure you meet course requirements.
  2. Choose courses that align with your career goals and interests.
  3. Seek advice from UMSI Academic Advising for personalized guidance.
