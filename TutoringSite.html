Tutoring Resources

Supporting Your Academic Journey

The School of Information Tutoring page offers a wealth of resources to aid students in their academic pursuits. From personalized tutoring sessions to comprehensive online materials, students can find the support they need to excel in their studies.

Available Tutoring Services:

  • Peer Tutoring - Engage in one-on-one tutoring sessions with fellow students who understand your challenges.
  • Online Resources - Check out our Academic Support Resources for instructional videos, study guides, and practice materials.

Additional Academic Support:

For further assistance with academic planning, reach out to our UMSI Academic Advising team, who can assist with course selections, career advice, and more.
