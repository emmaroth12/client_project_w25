Winter 2025 Important Dates

Key Academic Deadlines and Events

The Winter 2025 Dates page provides a clear and organized listing of important academic dates and deadlines for the semester. It includes key information such as the start and end dates of classes, holidays or breaks, registration deadlines, and the final exam schedule. The page is designed to help students stay on top of crucial deadlines.

Important Academic Dates:

  • Registration Opens: November 11, 2024
  • First Day of Classes: January 6, 2025
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day (No Classes): January 20, 2025
  • Last Day to Add/Drop Classes Without 'W': January 20, 2025
  • Midterm Examinations: February 24 - 28, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 3 - 9, 2025
  • Last Day to Withdraw from Classes with 'W': April 1, 2025
  • Final Examinations: April 21 - 25, 2025
  • End of Semester: April 25, 2025
  • Grades Due: May 1, 2025

These dates are crucial for your academic planning, ensuring that you meet all registration and course deadlines. Make sure to mark these dates on your calendar and reach out to UMSI Academic Advising if you have any questions.
